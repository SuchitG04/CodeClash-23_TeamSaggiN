Co-Curricular Activities

  • Debate Club

    The Debate Club provides a platform for students to develop their public speaking and critical thinking skills through engaging in debates on various topics.

  • Robotics Club

    The Robotics Club offers students the opportunity to learn about robotics and participate in competitions, fostering teamwork and problem-solving skills.

  • Art Club

    The Art Club encourages students to explore their creativity through various art forms, such as painting, drawing, and sculpture.

  • Drama Club

    The Drama Club allows students to express themselves through acting, directing, and stage production, while also building confidence and communication skills.

  • Music Club

    The Music Club provides a space for students to learn and practice various musical instruments, as well as participate in performances and concerts.
